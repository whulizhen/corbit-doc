https://groops-devs.github.io/groops/html/fundamentals.robustLeastSquares.html compute the observation redundancy R Ri = I - A*(A'*P*A)^(-1) * A' * P

L = A*X with lsq solution X = (A'*P*A)^(-1)*A'*P*L E = L - A*X E = Ri*L
