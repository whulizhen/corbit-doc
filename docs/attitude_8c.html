get attitude of the platform for ground receiver or spacecraft quaternion usually describes rotation between body fixed frame and ECI

参数
[in]index_objectindex of the satellite in the overall object list, can be used to distinguish GNSS satellites, LEO satellites and ground stations
[in]time_utctime in utc
[in]position_platform_ecfposition in ecf [m]
[in]velocity_platform_ecfvelocity in ecf [m/s]
[in]position_platform_eciposition in eci [m]
[in]velocity_platform_ecivelocity in eci [m/s]
[in]att_quaa pointer to the attitude quaternion from body fixed frame to the ECI frame
[out]exunit vector of x-axis in ECI
[out]eyunit vector of y-axis in ECI
[out]ezunit vector of z-axis in ECI
[out]epunit vector of solar panel normal in ECI
